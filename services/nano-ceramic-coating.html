What Is It

What Is Nano Ceramic Coating?

Nano ceramic coating is a liquid polymer — typically silicon dioxide (SiO2) — that chemically bonds with your vehicle's factory paint at the molecular level. Unlike traditional wax or sealants that sit on top of the paint, ceramic coating becomes part of the surface itself, creating a semi-permanent protective layer.

At Prestige Auto Shield, we use only the highest-grade ceramic coatings from global leaders in nano-technology. Our certified installers prepare and apply each coating in our climate-controlled studio, ensuring flawless results that protect and beautify your vehicle for years.

Key Benefits

Why Choose Ceramic Coating

Four powerful reasons Kuwait's most discerning drivers trust nano ceramic coating for their vehicles.

Hydrophobic Defense

Water, mud, and contaminants bead up and roll off effortlessly. Your car stays cleaner longer and dries faster after washing or rain.

UV Protection

Blocks 99% of harmful UV rays that cause paint fading, oxidation, and chalking. Your paint retains its deep, rich colour for years under Kuwait's intense sun.

Unmatched Gloss

Ceramic coating adds incredible depth and a rich, wet-look gloss that makes your paint appear deeper, richer, and more vibrant than the day it left the factory.

Long-Lasting Durability

Unlike wax that degrades in weeks, ceramic coating protects for 3–5 years with proper maintenance. Resistant to bird droppings, tree sap, acid rain, and road salts.

Our Process

The Application Process

Our meticulous 4-step process ensures a flawless, long-lasting bond between the coating and your paint.

01

Deep Wash & Decontamination

Every vehicle starts with a thorough two-bucket wash, iron decontamination, and clay bar treatment to remove all bonded surface contaminants.

02

Paint Correction

We machine-polish the paint to remove swirl marks, light scratches, and oxidation. A flawless base is essential — ceramic coating magnifies every existing imperfection.

03

Coating Preparation

Each panel is thoroughly wiped with a dedicated panel-prep solution to remove all oils, waxes, and polishing residues. The surface must be surgically clean for optimal bonding.

04

Coating Application & Curing

The ceramic coating is applied in cross-hatch patterns under controlled lighting. After application, the vehicle cures in our dust-free studio for 24–48 hours before delivery.

FAQ

Ceramic Coating FAQs

Depending on the product tier, nano ceramic coatings last between 3–5 years when properly maintained. Our premium coatings feature multi-layer SiO2 formulations backed by comprehensive manufacturer warranties. Regular maintenance washes and annual top-up inspections help maximize longevity.

Use a pH-neutral car shampoo and a microfiber wash mitt. Avoid automatic car washes with harsh brushes. We recommend a ceramic booster spray every 3–4 months to rejuvenate the hydrophobic properties. We provide a complete aftercare guide with every installation.

Pricing varies based on vehicle size, paint condition, and the coating tier selected. A standard sedan coating starts at 350 KWD, while full-size SUVs with multi-layer coatings range higher. We provide a detailed quote after assessing your vehicle. Contact us for a complimentary consultation.

Wax is a natural or synthetic substance that sits on top of the paint and typically lasts 4–8 weeks. Ceramic coating is a liquid polymer that chemically bonds with the paint at the molecular level, lasting 3–5 years. Ceramic coating offers superior hardness, chemical resistance, and UV protection compared to any wax or sealant.

They serve complementary purposes. PPF (Paint Protection Film) is a thick, impact-resistant film that protects against rock chips and physical damage. Ceramic coating provides chemical resistance, UV protection, and unmatched gloss. Many clients combine both — PPF on high-impact areas and ceramic coating over the entire vehicle for comprehensive protection.
