What is Engine Bay Detailing?

The Heart of Your Car

Engine bay detailing is a specialised service that goes far beyond a simple rinse. It involves careful degreasing, steam cleaning, and hand-dressing of every component under your hood — from the engine block and hoses to plastic covers and wiring looms. The result is a spotless, protected engine bay that looks as pristine as the day it left the factory.

We pair our deep cleaning with nano-ceramic coatings that protect plastic, rubber, and painted surfaces from UV damage, heat stress, and future dirt buildup. A detailed engine bay is easier to maintain, simpler to service, and significantly increases your vehicle's resale value.

Why Engine Bay Detailing

Four Pillars of Engine Care

Our engine bay detailing package delivers comprehensive cleaning and protection for every component, tailored to Kuwait harsh climate and driving conditions.

Deep Cleaning & Degreasing

We apply professional-grade degreasers and steam cleaners to dissolve years of oil residue, road grime, and baked-on dirt. Every crevice, hose, and bracket is meticulously scrubbed and rinsed to restore your engine bay to a like-new condition.

Nano-Ceramic Coating

A high-temperature nano-ceramic coating is applied to plastic, metal, and rubber surfaces. This invisible shield repels oil, dust, and moisture, making future cleaning as simple as a gentle rinse. The coating also protects against UV degradation and engine heat.

Corrosion Prevention

Kuwait's humidity and saline air accelerate corrosion on exposed metal components. Our treatment includes targeted anti-corrosion application on battery trays, alternators, brackets, and fasteners, extending the life of critical engine bay parts.

Maintenance & Leak Detection

A clean engine bay makes it easy to spot oil leaks, cracked hoses, and worn belts early. Our technicians document existing conditions and flag any potential issues we discover during detailing, giving you peace of mind and saving you costly repairs down the road.

Our Process

Thorough Engine Bay Care

Every engine bay undergoes our meticulous four-stage process to ensure complete cleaning and lasting protection without compromising sensitive components.

01

Assessment & Masking

We inspect the engine bay, identifying sensitive electronics, air intakes, and alternators. These components are carefully masked and sealed to prevent water ingress. We also note any pre-existing leaks or damage for your records.

02

Degrease & Agitate

We apply pH-balanced, biodegradable degreasers across all surfaces. Using detail brushes of various sizes, every hose, clamp, bracket, and crevice is hand-agitated to loosen embedded oil, grease, and dirt particles.

03

Pressure Rinse & Dry

A controlled low-pressure rinse removes all dissolved contaminants without forcing water into sensitive areas. The bay is then dried using compressed air and microfiber towels, reaching into every gap and around every component for spot-free results.

04

Dressing & Coating

Plastic and rubber components receive a UV-protectant dressing for a rich, like-new finish. Metal surfaces and painted components get a nano-ceramic coating that bonds at the molecular level, creating a durable hydrophobic barrier against future dirt and corrosion.
