What is Wash & Maintenance?

Professional Routine Care

Wash and maintenance is the foundation of any great vehicle preservation strategy. Unlike automatic car washes that use harsh brushes and recycled water, our hand wash process is a meticulous craft. Every vehicle receives the two-bucket method, premium microfiber mitts, and pH-neutral shampoos that preserve wax, sealant, and coating layers.

Our maintenance packages are designed to complement your existing protection — whether you have PPF, ceramic coating, or a traditional wax. Regular care extends the life of every protective layer and keeps your vehicle looking its absolute best between full detail sessions.

Maintenance Packages

Four Levels of Care

Choose the maintenance package that fits your lifestyle. From a quick refresh to complete care, every wash is performed with the same obsessive attention to detail.

Exterior Hand Wash

Two-bucket hand wash, premium wheel cleaning, tire dressing, and streak-free exterior glass. pH-neutral shampoos safe for all protection layers.

Interior Vacuum & Wipe

Thorough vacuuming of carpets, seats, and trunk. Dashboard, console, door panels, and surfaces wiped clean with appropriate care products.

Protection Boost

Clay bar treatment for paint decontamination, followed by a spray sealant or quick wax top-up. Restores water beading and gloss between full applications.

Full Maintenance Detail

Complete interior and exterior care — hand wash, clay bar treatment, sealant, interior detail, leather conditioning, and engine bay wipe-down. The ultimate between-detail maintenance.

Our Wash Process

Meticulous Wash Protocol

Every vehicle follows our strict wash protocol to ensure consistent, scratch-free results every time.

01

Inspection & Prep

We inspect the vehicle for existing damage, note areas requiring extra attention, and prepare the two-bucket wash station with grit guards.

02

Pre-Rinse & Foam

Thorough pre-rinse to remove loose dirt. pH-neutral foam is applied to encapsulate and lift contaminants before any physical contact.

03

Two-Bucket Hand Wash

Premium microfiber mitts and the two-bucket method ensure dirt is carried away, not dragged across the paint. Each panel is washed in sections.

04

Dry & Final Touch

Careful drying with plush waffle-weave towels using pat-and-blow technique. Glass polished streak-free, and final inspection ensures every surface is clean and protected.
