What Is Paint Correction

The Art of Perfection

Paint correction removes surface imperfections from your clear coat to restore a flawless, high-gloss finish using specialized multi-stage techniques.

Our master detailers assess your paint and execute a tailored correction plan delivering remarkable results.

Why Correct Your Paint

Benefits Beyond Appearance

Swirl Mark Removal

Eliminates unsightly swirl marks caused by improper washing, restoring clarity to your paint.

Gloss Restoration

Restores deep, wet-looking gloss that makes your vehicle's color pop.

Oxidation Removal

Removes oxidized paint layers from sun exposure, revealing vibrant original color.

Increased Value

A flawless finish significantly enhances your vehicle's resale value and appeal.

Our Process

How We Restore

01

Paint Inspection

We assess your paint under specialized lighting and measure clear coat depth.

02

Decontamination

Thorough decontamination and prep wash ensure a perfectly clean surface before correction.

03

Multi-Stage Correction

Using premium compounds and polishers, we remove imperfections layer by layer.

04

Final Protection

We apply a finishing wax, sealant, or ceramic coating to protect the corrected paint.
