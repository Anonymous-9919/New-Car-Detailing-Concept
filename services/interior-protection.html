What is Interior Protection?

Comprehensive Cabin Care

Interior Protection is a multi-layer system of specialized nano-coatings designed to safeguard every surface inside your vehicle. From premium leather upholstery and Alcantara to carpets, dashboards, and trim — each material receives a tailored protective solution that repels stains, blocks UV rays, and prevents premature wear.

Our advanced nano-polymers bond at the molecular level, creating an invisible barrier that keeps your interior pristine without altering its natural look, feel, or breathability. The result is a cabin that resists daily wear and stays showroom fresh for years.

Why Interior Protection

Four Layers of Defence

Our interior protection system delivers comprehensive coverage for every material in your cabin, engineered for Kuwait demanding climate and driving conditions.

Leather Protection

Premium nano-coating penetrates leather fibres, creating a stain-resistant barrier that prevents dye transfer, colour fading, and cracking while preserving the natural softness and breathability of your leather upholstery.

Fabric Shield

Advanced fluoropolymer technology encapsulates each fabric fibre in an invisible shield. Liquids bead up and roll off, while dirt and dust are easily vacuumed away without setting in. Perfect for carpets and fabric seats.

UV Protection

Blocks up to 99 per cent of harmful UV rays that cause dashboard fading, leather cracking, and premature aging of interior surfaces. Keeps your cabin cooler and extends the life of every component.

Odor Elimination

Photocatalytic and ozone treatments break down odour-causing bacteria, mould spores, and volatile organic compounds at the molecular level. Leaves your cabin smelling fresh and clean, not perfumed.

Our Process

Meticulous Interior Care

Every vehicle undergoes our meticulous four-stage process to ensure every surface is perfectly cleaned, prepped, and protected.

01

Deep Clean & Extract

Every surface is thoroughly vacuumed, steam-cleaned, and extracted. Carpets, seats, headliners, and crevices are freed of dirt, crumbs, and embedded debris using professional-grade extraction equipment.

02

Inspection & Prep

We inspect every material type — leather, fabric, vinyl, plastic, Alcantara — and select the precise protective chemistry for each surface. Sensitive areas are masked and protected before application.

03

Coating Application

Each protector is applied using calibrated techniques — spray, wipe, or buff — depending on the material. Nano-polymers bond at the molecular level for uniform, invisible coverage that does not alter texture or colour.

04

Cure & Final QC

The coatings cure for 12-24 hours in our controlled environment. We perform a final quality check under multiple lighting conditions, test water beading, and hand the car back with detailed aftercare instructions.
